$1,000 lump sum

Comparing scenarios: This page reports CAGR because all $1,000 is deployed at the window start. The annual DCA page reports IRR because its $100 deposits have different holding periods. Both are annualized rates and fair to compare on a per-dollar-of-time basis — but the lump sum keeps roughly 2× the dollar-years of capital invested across the window, so a lower lump-sum CAGR can still produce a higher terminal value than a seemingly better DCA IRR. Compare the terminal-value chart and the summary table alongside the rate, not the rate alone.
